Because why unwrap a real gift?

It was my friend’s Colin’s birthday tomorrow, but I didn’t have a gift. Instead of buying one, I decided to make a short video showing what life is like when he is out of town. No one to throw a football to on the beach, no one to share wings with at our favorite haunt.

I had a GoPro, a girlfriend willing to help, and six hours.
