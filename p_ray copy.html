What do you get a friend
who sees and reviews every movie?

Like me, my friend Ray loves movies. Unlike me, he loves sharing his opinion about them as soon as the credits roll, which explains their pithy punch. His brevity and opinions became infamous, so for his fortiest birthday I designed and had screen-printed tank tops for everyone to wear at a surprise party. Like the tank tops, his reviews are still going strong. And yeah, my friend Ray is a little short.

I can't wait for his 50th.
