Because why unwrap a real gift?

Instead of the usual gift — and because we had forgotten to buy one — my girlfriend and I challenged ourselves to make a birthday video for our friend, Colin, showing what life is like when he is out of town: No one to throw a football with on the beach; someone absent from our usual haunt of beers and wings. We had a GoPro, some sun, and eight hours before his party.

It was a fun, fast, and very informal endeavor. A small note. If you watch the video, the game 'wet balls' is as simple as its perhaps crude name: Keep the tennis ball in the air. Don't let it get wet.
